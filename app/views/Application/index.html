Conversationalist is an iPhone application which helps people have conversations with people they may not know as well. This is done by generating user related RSS feeds based on their online social footprint.

To ensure the highest level of user experience, we need to ensure that our recommendation engine for articles provides an good articles for users to use during their conversation.

This is the purpose of the analysis engine. We hope to fine-tune our algorithm by keeping track what you as users would choose as a potential conversation topic. All data will be completely anonymized and we are not storing any of your Facebook information.

Thank you for contributing to our research.
